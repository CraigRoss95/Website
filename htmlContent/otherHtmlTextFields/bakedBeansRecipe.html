• 3 cups dry navy beans boiled for one hour, then skimmed, and then drained
  • 1 ½ cups ketchup
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • ¼ cup molasses
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp dry mustard
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 6 slices thick cut bacon, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • ¾ cup brown sugar

Baked beans are a Bostonian staple and one of my favorites. This is a modified recipe that I use from AllRecipes that I got after some experimenting. Just throw everything above into a crockpot, mix it up until the homogeneous is homogonus, and let it sit on high for 6-10 hours. The longer you wait the sweeter and softer the beans will get.
