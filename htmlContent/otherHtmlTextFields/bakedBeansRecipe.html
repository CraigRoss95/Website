• 1 pound dried navy beans (re-hydrated)
  • 8 ounces uncooked bacon, diced
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1/3 cup molasses
  • 1/3 cup lightly packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar, or white vinegar /li>
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

Baked beans are a Bostonian staple and one of my favorites. This is a modified recipe that I use from some website I found that I got after some experimenting with different recipes. Just throw everything above into a crockpot, mix it up until thoroughly together, and let it sit on high for 8-10 hours. The longer you wait the sweeter and softer the beans will get, I have let these beans go over night and they still come out great. I also usually do a double recipe and freeze the extras in half recipe sizes, these beans are a great thing to pull out for summer BBQs
